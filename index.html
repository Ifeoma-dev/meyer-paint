ABOUT MEYER PAINT

MEYER PLC is the Manufacturer and Marketer of high quality Paints, including Architectural Paints, Wood Paints, Auto refinishes, Industrial and Marine, Road lining Paints, Roof coatings, Tube coatings, HP coatings and Adhesives. Our Company was incorporated on the 20th May, 1960 and converted to a Public Company in 1979. Meyer PLC is an offshoot of former Hagemeyer Nigeria Limited, the manufacturer of Sigma Paints and Cosmetics.
